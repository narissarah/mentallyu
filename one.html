What is Home-based therapy?

What is Home-based therapy?

In home based therapy, a therapist provides counselling to a patient at their own home. A lot of people have found success with home-based therapy. This form of therapy is helpful for people who have difficulty getting to private practices or mental health facilities on their own.

Advantage of Home-based therapy

Advantage of Home-based therapy

To ensure the best results, a therapeutic relationship should be conducted in the most secure environment possible in order to create an atmosphere of trust and comfort between client and therapist.

As a therapist, you may customize the office space to fit the patients needs, and yet your patient will never feel as safe or comfortable as in their own home. This is one of the advantages of home-based therapy. You may gain deeper insights from seeing how and where patients behave at home.

Ethical Concern

Ethical Concern

It is possible to have ethical concerns about home therapy, and these concerns include boundaries, confidentiality, or a sense of role confusion.
