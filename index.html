Mental health system

Mental health system

The problems with mental health system is that misdiagnoses happen in healthcare system. However, this information isn’t well known because there is no mechanism or system in place to keep in check. Nor is injustice reported because patients are self-conscious about their mental health issues. This is extremely dangerous because it keeps people from receiving the treatment that they needed. Patient also become confused and distraught when the course of treatment recommended isn’t working.

Don’t be too quick to come to a diagnosis

Don’t be too quick to come to a diagnosis.

Many common symptoms occur for a variety of reasons, and can have different diagnoses. That’s why you should go thorough evaluation based on a broad range of information before coming up with a diagnosis.

Also, sometime a misdiagnosed happens because a patient fails to disclose all their symptoms. They do not recognize certain emotions and behaviours as something revenant to their treatment, so it’s best to talk and learn more about your patients.

Help!! My Patient isn’t getting better

Help!! My Patient isn’t getting better

The thing is: If a patient isn’t responding to a particular course of treatment, it means that there need to be a re-evaluation of the diagnosis being given. Instead of changing therapist, some patients choose to stick with the same psychologist throughout their life.

What Can I do?

What Can I do?

Although having some from of stability and consistency is important, it’s okay to refer your patients to other psychologists. Sometimes transference and bias can obstruct a patient progress. There’s no tried and true method, so we should actively seek different methods that will help your patients.
